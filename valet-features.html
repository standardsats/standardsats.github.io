Valet Features

Valet is a unique Bitcoin wallet that gives you full control of your money and your financial life. Built for beginners and advanced users alike — with mobile Lightning node integration, privacy-focused features, and flexible controls. Like having your bank in your pocket, where you're the only one with the key.

Basic Features

Send and receive Bitcoin on-chain or instantly over the Lightning Network — small enough to pay for a coffee, or send money to friends and family.

Send Bitcoin

  • Bitcoin address — direct transfers to any standard Bitcoin address.
  • Lightning invoice — small, fast, cheap Bitcoin transfers.
  • Email — send to accounts linked via email (e.g. custodial accounts like Binance, Bybit).

Receive Bitcoin

  • Bitcoin address — generate addresses for incoming payments.
  • Lightning invoice — receive small amounts over the Lightning Network.

Advanced Features

Powerful, flexible controls for users who want more from their wallet.

Fiat Channels

Protects merchants against Bitcoin price volatility by locking in your bitcoins temporarily at the fixed Bitcoin price set when the deposit to the channel was made.

Custom Node Connection

Technically proficient users can connect their own Electrum/Bitcoin nodes to Valet for enhanced privacy — including operation over Tor.

Transaction Batching

Automatically assemble your transactions together with those of other users and send them as a single transaction, splitting the cost among everybody.

Coin Control (UTXO)

Choose which addresses (coins) to spend from in a transaction — helping lower fees and prevent dust balances.

Fee Control

Choose how much fee you want to pay for a transaction, directly affecting its confirmation speed.

Replace By Fee

Change the fee of an already-broadcast transaction — usually by raising it to prioritize the transaction in the mempool.

Security Features

Your keys, your coins — with layered protection for your device and your funds.

Recovery Phrase

A set of 12 or 24 English words created with your wallet that lets you recover your account if you lose access to your device.

Back Up

Automatic local backup files let you restore your Lightning channel connections by recovering your wallet.

PIN / Fingerprint

Biometric or numeric lock that prevents unauthorized access to the app on a lost or stolen device.

Hardware Connection

Connect Trezor or Ledger hardware wallets to Valet in Watch-Only mode to monitor them securely.
