Help Us Bring Self-Custodial Lightning to Everyone

Valet is a free, open-source Bitcoin & Lightning wallet built to work where others can't — on low-end phones, in low-bandwidth regions, with no custodian. We're seeking support to revive our Hosted/Fiat channels and keep Valet growing.

Project Summary

Valet is a fully open-source (Apache-2.0), non-custodial Bitcoin and Lightning wallet that puts a private, lightweight Lightning node in everyone's pocket — no full node, no pre-funded channels, no KYC. Built on our own mobile-first Immortan library, it runs on Android 8+ in under 30 MB and on minimal internet.

We're seeking grant support to continue building Valet, reviving our Hosted/Fiat channel technology, shipping new releases, and deepening adoption across the Global South. We'd be glad to discuss scope and timeline with any funder who wants to get involved.

The Problem

Bitcoin adoption is booming, but everyday use as a medium of exchange still lags, held back by real barriers:

  • ~1.7 billion adults are unbanked, largely cut off from traditional finance.
  • Bitcoin is the global alternative, but it's technical, and most wallets demand high-end devices, heavy bandwidth, or real skills.
  • Self-custodial Lightning is costly and confusing to set up for newcomers.
  • Bitcoin's price volatility scares merchants and pushes people toward centralized, censorable stablecoins.

What We're Building

Valet is designed for real people, not just power users — simple enough for a first-time user, light enough for a budget Android phone.

Runs anywhere

Android 8+, under 30 MB, minimal bandwidth, built for low-resource devices and regions.

Truly self-custodial

Seed-backed; your keys, your coins, no custodian, no KYC.

Full Lightning

Fast, low-cost payments straight from your device.

Privacy & control

Coin control, fee control, batching, private Electrum + Tor, watch-only/hardware support, local channel backups.

Simple by design

Clean UX with no jargon — you can even send and receive by email.

Open source

Apache-2.0 licensed — anyone can audit, fork, or rebuild it.

The Breakthrough: Hosted / Fiat Channels

Our standout innovation is zero-liquidity, anonymous Lightning channels you open just by scanning a Host's QR code. Fiat channels lock in a stable fiat value — a Bitcoin-backed alternative to centralized stablecoins — so merchants can accept Bitcoin without fearing volatility, while staying fully on Lightning. Anyone can run a Host, making it ideal for local Bitcoin communities.

This feature is currently paused due to infrastructure cost — reviving it is the heart of this grant.

Roadmap (Use of Funds)

With this grant we will:

  • Revive Hosted/Fiat channels and run the infrastructure behind them.
  • Ship new Immortan and Valet releases.
  • Extend Hosted/Fiat plugins beyond Eclair to LND and Core-Lightning.
  • Research BOLT12 and a cryptographic mitigation for the "Host exit-scam" risk.
  • Complete entity setup to return to Google Play.
  • Fund community education and localization across target regions.
